Who and what is McPhee creations?

Hi there!

Thanks for stopping by.

A little about me. I started out studying graphic design, enjoying but always had a fascination for websites. After some digital graphic design projects it did not take long before I was looking for more. After some time I decided to dedicate the majority of my time to web design & development. Not long after I found a position at an enterprise company.

At work I am full stack developer though the majority of my time is spent on the back end using Coldfusion language and SQL database - while also tackling various other tasks in other languages that pop up. Working there I have also been introduced to agile development and implement agile methodology in most of my projects.

Wanting to branch out from the 9-5, I started McPhee creations. Creating this I have a way to further develop my skills and pave my own path in where I would like to go. I am proficient with various JS frameworks & libraries including Jquery, React, Redux and more. Having the knowledge of backend systems and confident using Javascript I also use node js for various projects.

Work does take up most of my time but I always find time to dedicate to my passion which is Muay Thai/kickboxing. Not only do I find it incredibly fun but also gives me focus and discipline which aids in many parts of my life.
